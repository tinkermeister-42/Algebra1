## рҹ“ҳ LetвҖҷs Learn It

Negative numbers show direction or values below zero. You'll see them in banking, temperature, elevation, and more.

### вһ•вһ– Addition and Subtraction

- Same signs: Add and keep the sign  
  Example: -3 + (-2) = -5  
- Different signs: Subtract and keep the sign of the larger number  
  Example: -7 + 4 = -3  
  Example: 5 - 9 = -4  

рҹ’Ў Think of subtraction as *adding the opposite*  
Example:  
4 - (-3) = 4 + 3 = 7

### вң–пёҸвһ— Multiplication and Division

- Same signs вҶ’ Positive  
  Example: -2 Г— -6 = 12  
- Different signs вҶ’ Negative  
  Example: -8 Г· 2 = -4  

```{admonition} рҹ§  Example
Simplify: -5 - (-3) Г— 2

Step 1: Multiply вҶ’ -5 - (-6)  
Step 2: Subtract вҶ’ -5 + 6 = 1
```
