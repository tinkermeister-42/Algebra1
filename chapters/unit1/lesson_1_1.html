Lesson 1.1: Working with Negative Numbers#

Today we’re going to learn how to work with negative numbers — how to add, subtract, multiply, and divide them. This is a foundational skill that shows up in every part of algebra.

🎯 Objectives

  • Understand the meaning of negative numbers

  • Add and subtract positive and negative numbers

  • Multiply and divide with correct sign rules

Note

Vocab: integer, absolute value, opposite, additive inverse, product, quotient

🔥 Warm-Up#

Try the following problems. Some should feel familiar — others will get you thinking.

  1. What is the opposite of +5?

  2. Order from least to greatest: -4, 0, 3, -7

  3. What is the absolute value of -6?

  4. True or False: -(-8) = 8

📘 Let’s Learn It#

Negative numbers show direction or values below zero. You’ll see them in banking, temperature, elevation, and more.

➕➖ Addition and Subtraction#

  • Same signs: Add and keep the sign
    Example: -3 + (-2) = -5

  • Different signs: Subtract and keep the sign of the larger number
    Example: -7 + 4 = -3
    Example: 5 - 9 = -4

💡 Think of subtraction as adding the opposite
Example:
4 - (-3) = 4 + 3 = 7

✖️➗ Multiplication and Division#

  • Same signs → Positive
    Example: -2 × -6 = 12

  • Different signs → Negative
    Example: -8 ÷ 2 = -4

🧠 Example

Simplify: -5 - (-3) × 2

Step 1: Multiply → -5 - (-6)
Step 2: Subtract → -5 + 6 = 1

🤝 Try It Together#

Let’s work through these as a class:

  • -4 + (-7)

  • 3 - (-5)

  • -2 × 6

  • -10 ÷ (-2)

  • 8 - 12

📄 Practice On Your Own#

🌱 Level 1

  1. -6 + (-3)

  2. 4 - 9

  3. -2 × 4

🌿 Level 2

  1. -3 - (-5)

  2. -7 × (-2)

  3. 15 ÷ (-3)

🌳 Level 3

  1. -8 + 4 × 2

  2. -12 ÷ (-3) + (-2)

  3. 5 - (-3)²

Hint: Watch your order of operations carefully.

❌ Find the Mistake

  1. Leo says:
    -6 - (-4) = -10
    Is he right? Explain and correct it.

  2. Sofia writes:
    -2 × -3 = -6
    What mistake did she make?

✏️ Make Your Own

  1. Write two problems that involve adding or subtracting negative numbers.

  2. Write two problems that involve multiplying or dividing negative numbers.

Trade with a partner and solve each other’s!

🔁 Quick Review

  1. Simplify: |-7| + |3|

  2. Is -12 > -5? Why or why not?

  3. What’s the difference between -(-x) and -x?
